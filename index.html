EVERYTHING UP FRONT

these cards look soft and rounded on purpose - that's the app's own style crashing the punk party. this is what it actually feels like inside:

core

Full music player

Smart queue management, shuffle, repeat, skip - and skipping feels instant because the next tracks are already pre-cached in the background.

vibes

Live lyrics

Lyrics with auto-syncing and scrolling, following the song word by word while you pretend you knew them all along.

offline

Offline mode

Everything you listen to gets cached automatically. No internet? The smart queue skips straight to what's already on your device, so the music never stops.

yours

Recommendations you control

Discover new music based on your listening habits - and customize it fully. More of this, less of that, none of those. Your call, always.

import

Spotify + YouTube import

Bring your playlists over natively. Export your whole library to a JSON file and import it back whenever, wherever.

sync

Account sync

Your library, history and settings follow you across devices. Log in with Google or Discord - or don't log in at all, offline mode asks for nothing.

fast

Super fast streaming

The server caches every song and image it touches. Second time anyone asks, it streams straight from disk - that's how 17 seconds became 2.

system

Background playback

Native music notification with playback controls, built with hand-made plugins - the app keeps playing while you live your life.

library

A real library

Playlists, favorites, artists, albums, listening history, and a track context menu for everything - add to playlist, jump to album, you name it.

smart

Typo-proof search

Type "Nyrvana" or "Nir va na" - it still finds the artist. Fuzzy matching plus your own click history makes every search smarter.

space

Storage management

Set a cache limit in GB or a percentage of free space. The app trims itself automatically - delete songs, clear cache, see exactly what's used.

pretty

Themes & fonts

Themes, fonts, animations, accent colors... make it yours. The app has personality and lets you have yours too.

power

Self-hosting

Run your own server and stream from it, or use the public one. One URL in the settings, one switch, the whole app follows.

PRIVACY & DATA

what the server keeps

  • a random account id, your email, name and avatar (from google/discord login)
  • your app data - library, playlists, history, settings - so it syncs across devices
  • listening stats for recommendations - opt-out, wipeable, exportable
  • a shared song cache keyed by the song, not by you

what never happens

  • no ads, no ad SDKs, no ad ids
  • no selling or renting your data, ever
  • no IP logging, no request logs
  • no tracking across apps or sites
  • no password ever seen or stored
  • no cloud - one person's own hardware

"i don't say 'i take privacy very seriously'. i say: i have your data, and i only care to make sure it's safe from me and anyone else, in any way i can." - the whole policy is written like that, go read it, it's actually readable
