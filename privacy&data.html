PRIVACY & DATA

what the server keeps

  • a random account id, your email, name and avatar (from google/discord login)
  • your app data - library, playlists, history, settings - so it syncs across devices
  • listening stats for recommendations - opt-out, wipeable, exportable
  • a shared song cache keyed by the song, not by you

what never happens

  • no ads, no ad SDKs, no ad ids
  • no selling or renting your data, ever
  • no IP logging, no request logs
  • no tracking across apps or sites
  • no password ever seen or stored
  • no cloud - one person's own hardware

"i don't say 'i take privacy very seriously'. i say: i have your data, and i only care to make sure it's safe from me and anyone else, in any way i can." - the whole policy is written like that, go read it, it's actually readable
